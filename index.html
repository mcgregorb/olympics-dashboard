🇺🇸 USA Olympic Athletes

Jordan Stolz

Speed Skating
2x GOLD

20-year-old from Kewaskum, WI. Won 1000m and 5000m gold. Youngest American to win Olympic speed skating gold. World record holder.

Chloe Kim

Snowboard Halfpipe
SILVER

25-year-old from Torrance, CA. Two-time Olympic champion (2018, 2022). Took silver after upset by 17-year-old Choi Gaon. Competed through shoulder injury.

Tess Lemley

Freestyle Moguls
GOLD

Dominated women's moguls for Team USA's first freestyle gold of these Games. Led a strong American showing in the event.

Breezy Johnson

Alpine Skiing
GOLD

Won women's downhill gold earlier in the Games. DNF in Super-G on Day 6 amid difficult snowy and foggy conditions that saw 17 skiers fail to finish.

Jessie Diggins

Cross-Country Skiing
BRONZE

From Afton, MN. Bronze in women's 10km free. Has medaled in three consecutive Winter Olympics (2018 gold, 2022 silver, 2026 bronze).

Madison Chock & Evan Bates

Figure Skating - Ice Dance
SILVER

Married couple and three-time World champions. Won silver in ice dance in what may be their final Olympic appearance together.
