🇺🇸 USA Athlete Spotlights
CK
Chloe Kim • Snowboard Halfpipe🥈 Silver
Two-time Olympic champion (2018, 2022) earned silver after 17-year-old Choi Gaon's stunning upset. Kim, 25, from Torrance, CA, fell on her final run attempting a historic three-peat while competing through a torn labrum.
JS
Jordan Stolz • Speed Skating🥇 2x Gold
20-year-old from Kewaskum, WI became the youngest American to win Olympic speed skating gold. Won 1000m with an Olympic record of 1:06.28. Still has 500m and 1500m ahead.
IM
Ilia Malinin • Figure Skating🥇 Gold
The 21-year-old "Quad God" from Fairfax, VA dominated the men's free skate with a program featuring five clean quads, including the quad axel. First American men's singles gold since Evan Lysacek in 2010.
EL
Liz Lemley • Freestyle Moguls🥇 Gold
Led a dominant USA 1-2 finish with Jaelin Kauf (silver). First American woman to win moguls gold in 24 years since Shannon Bahrke in 2002.
BJ
Breezy Johnson • Alpine Skiing🥇 Gold
Won women's downhill gold after overcoming two ACL surgeries. Missed 2022 Beijing entirely due to injury. Her gold completed a four-year comeback journey.
JD
Jessie Diggins • Cross-Country🥉 Bronze
Gutted through bruised ribs for bronze in the 10km — first American to medal in XC at three consecutive Olympics (2018 gold, 2022 silver, 2026 bronze).
CB
Chock & Bates • Ice Dance🥈 Silver
Married couple and four-time World champions won emotional silver in their final Olympic appearance, narrowly losing to Papadakis/Cizeron.
RS
Cochran-Siegle • Alpine🥈 Silver
Vermont native earned back-to-back Olympic super-G silvers (2022, 2026). Part of the Cochran skiing dynasty — mother Barbara Ann won slalom gold in 1972.
